About

Let me introduce myself.

I was born and raised in Silicon Valley and moved to Oregon to attend college at the University of Oregon. I am now 25 years young living in Portland where I am earning a certificate for full stack web development.

Background

I first started coding around 12 or 13 when I taught myself the basics of html and css so I could personalise my MySpace page without being constrained by the premade layouts. My interest really kicked off in the midst of earning my economics degree in college, as we used several software systems to write code for economic purposes (make models, run regressions, forecast, etc). It was after taking an intro to CIS class that I realised I would change gears after finishing up my degree. I was fortunate to hear about the U of O bootcamp program from a friend, as I was considering going back for a second degree in CIS. The bootcamp has proven to be both intensive and challenging, and has prepared me for a lifelong journey of learning and growth.
